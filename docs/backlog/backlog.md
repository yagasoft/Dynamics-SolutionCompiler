## Next Review

- Keep new authoring expansion on the same export-backed rebuild bar that closed `B-009`, especially when widening beyond rebuild-safe authored views, entity-only site maps, and the current environment-variable shapes.
- Drive `B-010` first: use hybrid source-backed intent to close rebuild gaps for credible touched families and then keep promoting only the next structured authoring surfaces that clearly beat source-backed references, starting with the newer advanced UI and broader app-shell subset now proven through direct export reverse-generation and PAC pack.
- Treat `B-007` as the follow-on breadth stream again, but do not promote a later family into structured intent or richer reverse-generation until it clears the same omission-typed rebuild proof.
- Deepen reverse-generation only when the next family is already proven through canonical read, tracked/package emission, stable-overlap reread, and a clean classification in the omission report; keep unsupported tracked-source families explicit until that bar is met.
